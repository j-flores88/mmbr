FACIALS
  • THE MIRROR MIRROR MASTERPIECE $200

    Your skin is uniquely created & this facial is designed to honor that. Whether you're dealing with dryness, dullness, sensitivity, hyperpigmentation, uneven texture, anti-aging, or mild acne. It includes a double cleanse, exfoliation, extractions, luxury mask, facial + decollate/arm massage, hydro or diamond machine treatment (optional), targeted serums & finishing hydration with products chosen just for you. Perfect for monthly maintenance or as your go-to treatment.

  • THE BACK REFINEMENT PEEL $180

    An intensive resurfacing treatment designed to target stubborn back acne, hyperpigmentation, and uneven texture. We perform a thorough skin assessment to select the ideal peel strength to clarify, smooth, and renew the skin. This service is the ultimate architectural reset for a clear, confident complexion.

    Important Preparation Pre-Treatment Protocol: To ensure the safety and efficacy of your treatment, please discontinue the use of all retinols and chemical exfoliants on the back area at least one week prior to your appointment.

  • SKIN RESURFACING PEEL $160

    A professional-grade, custom-blended resurfacing treatment designed to target sun damage, hyperpigmentation, fine lines, and acne. We begin with a skin assessment to determine the ideal peel strength for your unique needs, ranging from a gentle surface renewal to a more intensive, deeper treatment. This service improves overall skin tone and texture for a refined, luminous finish.

    Important Preparation Pre-Treatment Protocol: To ensure the safety and efficacy of your treatment, please discontinue the use of all retinols and chemical exfoliants at least one week prior to your appointment.

  • BACK REFINEMENT FACIAL $120

    The ultimate facial treatment for the back. We begin with a skin assessment to design a custom blueprint for your body’s needs. This service includes deep cleansing, exfoliation, and targeted treatments to clarify pores, improve skin texture, and reveal a smoother, brighter complexion. The perfect architectural reset for skin that is often forgotten.
